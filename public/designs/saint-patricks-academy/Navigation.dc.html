About Us
“For God and Country”
Our motto since 1965

Six decades of
shaping young minds

Founded in 1965, St. Patrick's Academy has grown into one of Meerut's most respected schools — a green twelve-acre campus where rigorous academics meet a vibrant house culture, sport and the arts. Every Patrician leaves us confident, curious and grounded in values that last a lifetime.

A Message & Vision

From the Principal's Desk

At St. Patrick's, education is never only about results. Our motto — For God and Country — is a daily reminder that we are raising not just scholars, but citizens of conscience: young people who lead with integrity, serve with compassion, and carry their values into everything they do.

Our vision is a school where every child is known, challenged and cared for — where tradition gives us roots and a future-ready education gives us wings. I warmly invite you to visit our campus and see that spirit for yourself.
