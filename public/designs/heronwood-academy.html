Section 02 — Our Mission
"We are not in the business of filling pails. We tend fires — and we have done so, on this river, for one hundred and twenty-seven years."

Heronwood was founded on a simple, stubborn idea: that children are not problems to be managed but people to be trusted. We keep our classes small so that no one disappears. We keep our standards high because young people rise to what we ask of them.

The result is a graduate who reads closely, argues honestly, makes things with their hands, and carries themselves into the world with curiosity intact.

Eleanor Vance Head of School
