About the school

An education built on substance, not show.

For more than a hundred years, St. Patrick's has balanced academic ambition with genuine care. Our classrooms are small, our standards are high, and every child is known by name.

We believe character is taught as deliberately as calculus — through service, sport, the arts, and the everyday culture of a school that expects the best and brings it out.
